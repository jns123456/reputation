Frequently asked questions

How scoring works, what we are not, and how reputation and popularity stay separate. For the big picture, see About.

Reputation

Resolved forecasts only. Based on Polymarket odds at the time you predicted.

Popularity

Upvotes (+1) and downvotes (−1) on comments and forecasts. Never affects reputation.

Reputation scoring

When a market resolves, your formal forecast is scored using the market probability at the moment you submitted it:

Correct → + (100 − market %)  ·  Incorrect → − market %

Consensus pick — market at 90% Yes

You forecast Yes. Polymarket already priced it as very likely.

Correct: +10 Wrong: −90

Contrarian call — market at 10% Yes

You forecast Yes against the crowd. Higher reward, lower penalty if wrong.

Correct: +90 Wrong: −10

Coin flip — market at 50%

Balanced odds mean symmetric stakes either way.

Correct: +50 Wrong: −50

Every change creates an auditable event record with a human-readable reason. Resolved forecasts cannot be deleted.
