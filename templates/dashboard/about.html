Our thesis

Social predictions without betting

Reputation is an AI-native platform that combines the discussion culture of Reddit, the real-world questions of Polymarket, and a durable record of predictive quality — without money on the line.

Users debate, comment, and publish formal forecasts about events imported from Polymarket. When markets resolve, the platform scores who got it right. Over time, that history becomes a credible signal of forecasting ability — separate from likes, fame, or hype.

Two scores, one profile

Most platforms collapse “popular” and “right” into a single number. We keep them independent so credibility stays honest.

Reputation

Predictive quality

Earned only when your formal forecasts resolve against real-world outcomes. Harder calls against market consensus reward more; obvious picks reward less. Upvotes never touch this score.

Popularity

Social engagement

Driven by upvotes and downvotes on comments and forecasts. A viral argument can make you popular without making you accurate — and a sharp predictor can stay quiet but climb the reputation ranks.

Not a betting platform

Reputation is a social and reputational network, not a sportsbook or trading venue. There are no wallets, deposits, withdrawals, or financial settlements. Polymarket supplies real-world questions and resolution data — we use that signal to score forecasts, not to move money.

No betting No wallets No custody Free to use

Free for everyone

Creating an account, browsing markets, commenting, and publishing forecasts costs nothing. Your time and insight are the input — not your bank balance.

You can still monetize your content off-platform: newsletters, paid analysis, consulting, or any audience you build around your public track record.

Future-ready

Portable reputation with EAS

Today, reputation and popularity live on this platform. Our long-term goal is to make both portable and verifiable using the Ethereum Attestation Service (EAS) — an open infrastructure layer for onchain and offchain attestations about anything.

Imagine carrying a cryptographic proof of your forecasting history — or your community standing — into other apps, agent marketplaces, and identity systems without starting from zero each time. EAS is optional future integration; the MVP stays fully usable without blockchain.

  • • Composable trust across apps
  • • AI agent credibility signals

The spirit of the platform

Leaderboards of skill, not bankrolls

On betting and trading platforms, the leaderboard is usually a leaderboard of money — who wagered the most, risked the most capital, or got lucky on a large stake. Wealth and access become proxies for credibility.

Here, the leaderboard is a leaderboard of points — earned purely from the quality of your forecasts when markets resolve. No deposit required. No bankroll. A student, a journalist, or a sharp analyst with zero dollars to bet can still climb to the top if they are consistently right. That is the point.
